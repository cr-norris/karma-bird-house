The Karma Bird House team is made up of a group of friends and collaborators who maintain, program, and manage the building for continued public and private uses. Their common purpose is create a place where people feel inspired, safe, and part of a community. The team consists of building owners Michael and Giovanna Jager, supported by Chris & Jasmine and their studio, Alder. With the help of several like-minded volunteers, building members and collaborators, Karma has continued to produce hundreds of happenings, openings, parties, and experiences for over a decade.
